Day 1 - Arrival in Hpa-An
July 03, 2025

Settle into the peaceful rhythms of Hpa-An, a charming town surrounded by dramatic limestone mountains. Take a leisurely walk along the Thanlwin River and soak in the serene atmosphere. Enjoy a local meal while getting a first glimpse of the region’s unique culture and warm hospitality.

Day 2 - Caves & Cultural Wonders
July 04, 2025

Explore the famous Sadan Cave, filled with Buddha images and natural rock formations. Continue to Kawgun Cave, known for its ancient carvings and hidden shrines. End the day at Yathaypyan Cave, catching panoramic views of the surrounding countryside at sunset.

Day 3 - Mount Zwegabin Adventure
July 05, 2025

Embark on a hike up Mount Zwegabin, one of Hpa-An’s most iconic landmarks. Pass by monasteries, stairways, and monkeys as you ascend for sweeping views from the top. Descend and relax at Lumbini Garden, where hundreds of Buddha statues line the paths.

Day 4 - Village Life & Waterfalls
July 06, 2025

Visit a nearby village to experience local life, traditions, and crafts. In the afternoon, cool off at the refreshing Kyone Htaw Waterfall, a popular spot for locals. Enjoy a quiet evening in Hpa-An town with street food or a riverside dinner.

Day 5 - Relaxation & Departure
July 07, 2025

Spend your final morning at leisure—perhaps browsing a local market or enjoying a slow breakfast with mountain views. Reflect on the journey before departing with unforgettable memories of Hpa-An’s nature and culture.
