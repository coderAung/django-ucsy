How Can We Help You?

Find answers to common questions or reach our support team directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour package?

Booking a tour package is simple! Browse our destinations, select your preferred package, choose your travel dates, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide traveler details and make a payment to confirm your booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies by package. Generally, cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a full refund minus a processing fee. Between 15-29 days, we refund 50%. Cancellations within 14 days of departure are non-refundable. Please check specific terms for your package.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we highly recommend purchasing travel insurance for all bookings. We offer comprehensive insurance options that cover trip cancellations, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and other travel-related issues. You can add insurance during the booking process.

How can I modify my existing booking?

You can modify your booking by logging into your account and accessing the "My Trips" section. For changes to travel dates, destinations, or traveler information, please contact our support team as soon as possible. Modification fees may apply depending on the changes and how close you are to your travel date.

What documents do I need for Myanmar travel?

For international travel, you typically need a valid passport (with at least 6 months validity), visas for your destination country, and any required vaccinations. We provide detailed documentation requirements for each destination after booking, but we recommend checking with the relevant embassy for the most current information.

Can I customize a tour package?

Yes! Many of our packages can be tailored to suit your preferences. You can request changes to the itinerary, accommodation, or activities. Contact our support team, and we’ll help design a trip that fits your needs.

Do you offer group discounts?

Absolutely! We offer special discounts for groups of 8 or more travelers. Please reach out to our team for a custom quote based on your group size and destination.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and secure online payment gateways. For some destinations, we also offer installment payment plans — just ask our team!

Are airport transfers included?

Many of our packages include airport pickup and drop-off. If not included, you can add this service during checkout or request it separately by contacting us.

Do I need vaccinations for my trip?

Some destinations require specific vaccinations or health certificates. We’ll inform you about recommended vaccines after booking, but we also advise checking official health guidelines for your destination.

Can I travel solo on your tours?

Yes! We welcome solo travelers. Some packages may include a single supplement fee, but we also have group tours where you can share rooms with other travelers to save costs.

How do I contact support during my trip?

We provide 24/7 customer support via phone, email, and live chat. You’ll receive emergency contact details in your travel documents, so help is always just a call away.
